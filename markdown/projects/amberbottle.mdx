student of Navrachana School visited primary government schools to teach students how to make paper from scratch using old waste paper. During the activity, the student explained how recycling paper helps save trees, reduce waste, and protect the environment. This hands-on session encouraged young students to understand the importance of sustainability and practice eco-friendly habits in their daily lives.

#### 1. Promotes recycling   
Paper making uses waste paper, reducing the need for cutting trees and encouraging reuse of resources.

#### 2. Eco-friendly learning  
Students learn how everyday waste can be turned into useful products without harming the environment.

#### 3. Builds responsibility
The activity develops awareness about sustainability and motivates students to adopt green habits in daily life.
