Get ready for a fun and meaningful journey toward a greener lifestyle! This upcoming project encourages students to track their daily waste, become more mindful of their habits, and take small steps toward reducing it. The best part? As your waste goes down, you unlock cute, eco-themed badges that celebrate your progress. It’s learning, action, and rewards—all rolled into one exciting experience that turns sustainability into a game everyone will love.

#### 1. Track your waste
Students will record their daily waste to understand how much they generate and identify ways to reduce it.

#### 2. Reduce The Clog
By making small changes, students can actively lower their waste levels and contribute to a cleaner environment.
